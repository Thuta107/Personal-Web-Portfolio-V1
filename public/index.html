Wh🤔 Am I ?
Hello! My name is Thuta Lin and creating and building things has always been my passion. In 2016, I discovered programmin as part of my Engineering Foundation Studies at Curtive University Malaysia. With the new-found love for programming, I decided to pursue Software Engineering Program at Monash University Malaysia in 2017 of July.

Throughout the program, I was given the opportunity to learn various:
  • Programming Concepts & Paradigms
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Software Development & Project Management
  • Quality Assurance (QA) & Testing
  • Data Science & AI

In addition, the university environment gives me a chance to improve and hone on my soft skills: Communication, Leadership, Conflict Resolution & Team Work.

After four-year of studies, I finally graduated in 2021 and looking forward to make a career as a successful Software Engineer. Feel free to check out my resume (below) & portfolio to get to know me better. Please do keep in touch if you are looking to hire or if you just want a quick chat.
